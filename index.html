Hi, I'm Jessie

I'm a life-long learner who has been lucky enough to travel to many places.

I was born and raised in Poughkeepsie, NY then moved to NYC for college. I studied abroad in Spain, then transferred to American University in Washington, D.C. I studied once more in Irbid, Jordan for a summer semester before graduating with a degree in International Relations.

While studying abroad in Spain, I learned Spanish and was fortunate enough to visit Morocco, Italy, France and other parts of Spain. Once I finished my summer semester in Jordan, I was able to spend a couple of weeks traveling around Israel and then about a month in Europe.

After college, I decided to move to Europe where I got certified to teach English as a foreign language in Prauge. I then worked in Croatia for a summer at an international camp before moving to Berlin for a year where I taught English to children at a private bilingual pre-school and to adults at a continuing education language school.
